Instructions

Use your cursor to click around and collect coordinates. A coordinate is captured and outputted to the text area as you click.

The Buttons

1. Output new-line / continuous

Allows you to define if you want your coordinates to output individually as a new

0,0
0,1
0,2

or as a continuous comma-separated string.

0,0,0,1,0,2

2. Set image

Upload your own image to use. Your file is not sent anywhere, it is all handled locally on the client side.

3. Clear coordinates

Clear the coordinate output box!

4. Show trace

Shows you an overlay representing your trace. This feature is not available in new-line output mode, because I'm lazy.

5. Copy coordinates

Sends the coordinates from the output to your clipboard to paste anywhere!

That's it :-)
